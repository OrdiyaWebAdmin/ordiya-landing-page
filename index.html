How It Works

Four steps. Zero friction.

From scribbled note to scheduled event in seconds — no typing required.

Step 1

Snap

Take a photo of your handwritten notes, post-it, or planner page. Our OCR engine reads your handwriting instantly.

Step 2

Speak

Add context with your voice — "Schedule this for Thursday at 2pm" or "Remind me the morning of." Natural language, naturally.

Step 3

Sync

Ordiya creates the event and syncs it across Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook — automatically.

Step 4

Done

Sit back. You'll get a smart notification at just the right time. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know about Ordiya.

Our OCR engine is trained on millions of handwriting samples and achieves over 95% accuracy across most handwriting styles. It handles cursive, print, mixed case, and even the messiest notes. The AI also improves with use — the more you scan, the better it gets at reading your specific handwriting.
Ordiya integrates with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar (iCal), and Microsoft Outlook. We support two-way sync so changes made anywhere are reflected everywhere. More integrations (Notion, Todoist, Fantastical) are on our roadmap.
