Paper is fast.
ORDIYA makes it useful.

The good stuff rarely begins in a perfect form. It starts on a receipt, in a notebook, or as something you say before you forget. ORDIYA keeps the original context and gives it structure.

Capture your way

Use the input that fits the moment.

No single workflow forced onto every note. ORDIYA treats photos, voice, and typed entries differently so the result stays readable.

01 · Photograph

Turn paper into something usable.

Capture a receipt, handwritten list, or planner page. Review the extracted text before saving it.

02 · Speak

Say it before it disappears.

Record a natural voice note, keep the full transcript, and reopen it whenever you need the complete thought.

03 · Organize

Save it to the day it belongs.

Choose a date first, then type, photograph, or record. The finished entry remains connected to that day instead of getting lost in a generic inbox.
